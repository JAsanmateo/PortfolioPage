About Me

My name is Jose San Mateo, a current student at the UC Berkeley Extension Coding bootcamp. I am an aspiring full-stack developer with experience in HTML,CSS and Javascript.

Before learning how to do web development I was a journalist in the San Francisco Bay Area. I covered everything from Video games to local high school sports. Some of my writing credits include articles in the Vallejo Times-Herald Newspaper, Games Radar and GameRant among others.

My current passion outside of coding and web development is streaming video games on twitch where I host a regular show. My focus is primarily streaming fighting games and am part of a community of streamers known as the Campfire Stream Team
